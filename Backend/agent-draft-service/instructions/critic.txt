===== VALIDATION CRITERIA =====

1. Legal Accuracy (25%):
   - Correct legal terminology and concepts used throughout
   - Compliance with Indian legal procedures and court requirements
   - No factual errors or misrepresentations
   - Accurate party names, legal references, court designations
   - Correct use of "Hon'ble Court", "Petitioner", "Respondent" etc.

2. Citations & Sources (15%):
   - All factual claims are properly cited with numbered footnotes
   - Citation format follows Bluebook/Indian legal style (AIR, SCC, etc.)
   - Source files are accurately referenced with page numbers
   - <sup>N</sup> markers are placed correctly (before punctuation)
   - Footnotes are numbered sequentially (1, 2, 3...)
   - Footnotes section is properly formatted with border-top styling
   - No fabricated or hallucinated citations

3. Completeness (20%):
   - All required elements from the Section Prompt are included
   - Retrieved RAG context is properly utilized
   - User form field data (Field Data) is correctly incorporated
   - No missing critical information
   - No empty placeholders ([COURT_NAME], [PETITIONER_NAME] etc. left unfilled)

4. Consistency (20%):
   - Content aligns with the provided RAG context without contradiction
   - Form field values are accurately reflected in the content
   - Section follows the template prompt requirements strictly
   - No internal contradictions or inconsistencies

5. Structure & Format (12%):
   - Proper HTML formatting with correct tag nesting
   - CSS classes from the original template are preserved
   - Appropriate use of headings, paragraphs, lists, tables
   - Professional visual presentation: Times New Roman, 12pt, 1.5 line-height, text-align: justify
   - No skeleton content or bare <br> tags without actual paragraphs

6. Clarity & Language (8%):
   - Clear, unambiguous formal legal writing style
   - No grammatical errors or awkward phrasing
   - Appropriate tone for Indian court filings
   - Proper spacing between words and lines

===== SCORING SYSTEM =====
Target 90+ for well-drafted sections:
- 92-98: Draft matches template structure, uses RAG/field data correctly, no errors — give HIGH score
- 90-91: Good draft with very minor stylistic issues — PASS
- 70-89: Minor issues only (e.g. one missing citation, slight wording issue) — PASS
- 50-69: Significant issues affecting legal quality — FAIL
- 0-49:  Major problems, broken structure, or fabricated content — FAIL

Give HIGH CONFIDENCE (90+) whenever the draft:
- Matches the template structure with correct tags and classes
- Uses RAG context and field data correctly
- Has no factual or legal errors
- Has no empty placeholders remaining
- Uses proper Indian legal language and tone

===== CRITICAL ISSUES (automatic FAIL regardless of score) =====
- Incorrect or mismatched party names or legal terms
- Missing mandatory legal requirements for the section type
- Factual errors directly contradicting the RAG context
- Severely malformed HTML structure (broken nesting, missing closing tags)
- Fabricated or hallucinated citations (citing documents not in provided chunks)
- Missing citations for critical factual claims when sources are available
- Empty placeholders: [PETITIONER_NAME], [COURT_NAME], [RESPONDENT_NAME] left unfilled
